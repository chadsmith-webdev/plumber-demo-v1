About Razorback Plumbing

Locally owned. In business since 2009. We fix pipes and we don't make you guess what it'll cost.

Our story

Started with a truck and a phone

Jake Dawson started Razorback Plumbing in 2009 with one work truck and a cell phone that rang more than he expected. He'd been working for a larger outfit in Northwest Arkansas for eight years and kept hearing the same thing from customers: nobody answers, nobody shows up on time, and nobody tells you the price until the bill shows up.

So he went out on his own. The idea was simple: answer the phone, show up when you say you will, and tell people what it costs before you start working. That's still how we operate.

We've grown since then. There are more trucks now and a few more people. But the approach hasn't changed. We're still a local crew doing plumbing for our neighbors in Benton and Washington counties.

How we run this business

Answer the phone

When you call, someone picks up. We know that when you need a plumber, you need a plumber. Not a voicemail box.

Show up on time

If we say we'll be there at 10, we'll be there at 10. Your time matters and we're not going to waste it.

Tell you the price

You get a written estimate before we start. The number on the estimate is the number on the invoice. No surprises, no add-ons.
